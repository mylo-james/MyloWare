# TikTok Community Guidelines

What's allowed, what's banned, and how to avoid shadowbans.

---

## Content Categories

### Prohibited Content (Immediate Removal)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Violence** | Gore, graphic injury, torture |
| **Hate speech** | Slurs, discrimination, dehumanization |
| **Harassment** | Bullying, threats, doxxing |
| **Sexual content** | Nudity, explicit acts, sexualization of minors |
| **Dangerous activities** | Harmful challenges, self-harm promotion |
| **Illegal activities** | Drug sales, weapons trafficking |
| **Misinformation** | Election fraud claims, dangerous health misinformation |
| **Spam** | Fake engagement, bot activity |

### Restricted Content (Limited Distribution)

| Category | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Violent video games | Age-restricted, not on FYP for minors |
| Alcohol/tobacco | Not shown to under-18 |
| Gambling | Restricted visibility |
| Cosmetic procedures | May require age gate |
| Sensitive topics | Reduced FYP distribution |

## Shadowban Triggers

### What Causes Shadowbans

| Behavior | Risk Level |
|----------|------------|
| Community guideline violations | 游댮 High |
| Repeated borderline content | 游댮 High |
| Spam behavior (mass follow/unfollow) | 游댮 High |
| Fake engagement schemes | 游댮 High |
| Watermarks from competitors | 游리 Medium |
| Duplicate/reposted content | 游리 Medium |
| Controversial political content | 游리 Medium |
| Excessive hashtag spam | 游리 Medium |
| New account posting rapidly | 游 Low-Medium |

### Signs of Shadowban

- Zero or near-zero FYP views
- Views only from followers
- Videos not appearing in hashtag feeds
- Sudden drop from normal view counts

### Recovery

1. Stop any violating behavior
2. Delete flagged content if identified
3. Take 1-3 day posting break
4. Resume with safe, high-quality content
5. Gradually rebuild (may take 2-4 weeks)

## AI Content Rules

### Required Disclosure

| Content Type | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| Deepfakes of real people | Must disclose as synthetic |
| AI-generated faces | Recommended disclosure |
| AI voice cloning | Must disclose if mimicking real person |
| AI avatars | Acceptable without disclosure |

### What's Allowed

- AI-generated art and visuals
- AI-enhanced effects
- AI avatars and characters (fictional)
- AI backgrounds and environments

### What's Banned

- Deepfakes for deception
- Non-consensual AI likeness
- AI misinformation (fake events, fake quotes)
- AI content depicting minors inappropriately

## Age-Sensitive Content

### Under-18 Protections

- Restricted FYP for minors
- No DMs from strangers
- Limited duet/stitch options
- Enhanced privacy defaults

### Content Depicting Minors

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| Kids in family content | Minors in dangerous situations |
| Educational content | Romantic/dating content with minors |
| Age-appropriate activities | Sexualization in any form |

## Safety Best Practices

### Avoid These Caption/Content Patterns

| Pattern | Why It's Risky |
|---------|----------------|
| "Unalive" workarounds | May still be flagged |
| Violence aestheticization | Even if fictional |
| Substance glorification | Even if legal |
| Extreme diet content | Eating disorder triggers |
| Self-harm references | Even awareness content |

### Safe Alternatives

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| Graphic violence | Implied or cutaway |
| Dangerous stunts | "Don't try this" + safety context |
| Controversial opinions | Balanced presentation |
| Shock value | Creative hooks instead |

## Appeals Process

### If Content is Removed

1. Go to inbox -> System notifications
2. Find the removal notice
3. Tap "Appeal"
4. Provide context (brief)
5. Wait 24-48 hours for review

### Appeal Success Factors

- Misunderstanding by AI moderation
- Context wasn't clear
- Educational/documentary purpose
- Satire/parody that was misread

### Appeal Failure Factors

- Actual guideline violation
- Repeated same offense
- Clear intent to violate

## Account Health

### Strikes System

| Strikes | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Warning, content removed |
| 2 | Temporary features restriction |
| 3 | Longer restriction |
| 4+ | Potential permanent ban |

### Maintaining Good Standing

- Read guidelines thoroughly
- Don't push boundaries repeatedly
- Delete questionable old content
- Respond to warnings immediately
- Don't create alt accounts to evade bans
