# Landscape Video Styles Guide

Reference guide for creating cinematic landscape videos using AI video generation.

---

## Landscape Categories

### Mountains
- Snow-capped peaks at sunrise/sunset
- Misty mountain valleys
- Alpine meadows with wildflowers
- Dramatic cliff faces and ridges
- Mountain lakes reflecting peaks

### Ocean & Beach
- Gentle waves on sandy beach
- Dramatic crashing waves on rocks
- Sunset/sunrise over calm water
- Aerial views of coastline
- Underwater light rays

### Forest & Woods
- Sunlight filtering through canopy
- Misty forest paths
- Autumn leaves falling
- Ancient redwood groves
- Rainforest waterfalls

### Desert & Plains
- Sand dunes at golden hour
- Storm clouds over prairie
- Canyon formations with dramatic shadows
- Starry night time-lapses
- Wind-swept grasslands

### Sky & Weather
- Dramatic cloud formations
- Northern lights (aurora)
- Lightning storms (distant)
- Hot air balloons at sunrise
- Sun rays breaking through clouds

## Video Prompt Writing

### Effective Prompt Structure

```
[Camera movement] + [Subject] + [Location/Setting] + [Lighting] + [Atmosphere] + [Quality modifiers]
```

### Example Prompts

**Mountain Sunrise**
```
Cinematic drone shot slowly ascending over majestic snow-capped mountain peaks at golden hour, soft pink and orange clouds drifting between summits, warm morning light casting long shadows, 4K ultra HD, nature documentary style, smooth slow motion
```

**Ocean Sunset**
```
Peaceful tracking shot along pristine sandy beach at sunset, gentle waves rolling onto shore, warm golden and purple sky reflecting on calm water, dreamy soft focus atmosphere, cinematic color grading, slow motion 0.5x speed
```

**Forest Light**
```
Smooth dolly shot through ancient forest, volumetric sun rays filtering through misty canopy, dappled light on forest floor, magical ethereal atmosphere, shallow depth of field, cinematic 24fps
```

**Desert Golden Hour**
```
Sweeping aerial view over rolling sand dunes at sunset, long dramatic shadows, warm orange and red tones, wind gently moving sand particles, epic scale, IMAX quality cinematography
```

## Camera Movements

| Movement | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Slow push-in | Creates intimacy | Revealing details |
| Ascending drone | Shows scale | Mountains, canyons |
| Tracking shot | Smooth flow | Beaches, paths |
| Static wide | Contemplative | Sunsets, storms |
| Slow pan | Surveying scene | Panoramas |

---

## Lighting Conditions

### Golden Hour (Best for Motivation Videos)
- 30 min after sunrise / before sunset
- Warm, soft, flattering light
- Long shadows add depth
- Colors: gold, orange, pink

### Blue Hour
- Just before sunrise / after sunset
- Cool, mysterious mood
- Great for reflection scenes
- Colors: blue, purple, pink

### Overcast
- Soft, even lighting
- No harsh shadows
- Good for forests, waterfalls
- Colors: muted, soft

---

## Quality Modifiers for Prompts

Include these for best results:
- "cinematic" - Film-like quality
- "4K ultra HD" - High resolution
- "slow motion" - Dreamy feel
- "nature documentary style" - Professional look
- "smooth camera movement" - No shake
- "volumetric lighting" - Light rays
- "shallow depth of field" - Blurred background
