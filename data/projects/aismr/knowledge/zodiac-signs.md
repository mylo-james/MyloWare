## Element Groups

### Fire Signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
- Visual themes: Flames, sparks, heat shimmer, volcanic, crystalline fire
- Colors: Red, orange, gold, ember
- Textures: Crackling, sharp, radiating

### Earth Signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)
- Visual themes: Stone, moss, crystals, soil, ancient materials
- Colors: Green, brown, terracotta, obsidian
- Textures: Rough, organic, geometric, grounded

### Air Signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
- Visual themes: Smoke, mirror, light, electricity, wind
- Colors: Silver, white, iridescent, electric blue
- Textures: Reflective, shifting, weightless

### Water Signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)
- Visual themes: Liquid, bubbles, mist, deep sea, rain
- Colors: Blue, teal, purple, pearl
- Textures: Flowing, translucent, soft

## Surreal Modifier Ideas by Element

### Fire-Aligned Modifiers
- Crystal (sharp, faceted, light-catching)
- Lava (glowing, molten, flowing heat)
- Ember (glowing edges, smoldering)
- Solar (radiant, warm, golden)

### Earth-Aligned Modifiers
- Moss (soft, green, organic growth)
- Stone (ancient, weathered, solid)
- Clay (moldable, earthy, terracotta)
- Mineral (geode, layered, geological)

### Air-Aligned Modifiers
- Mirror (reflective, chrome, dual)
- Cloud (soft, floating, ethereal)
- Smoke (wispy, shifting, translucent)
- Electric (crackling, neon, pulsing)

### Water-Aligned Modifiers
- Slime (glossy, flowing, satisfying)
- Bubble (transparent, floating, delicate)
- Ice (frozen, crystalline, melting)
- Liquid (mercury, water, flowing)

## Cross-Element Surreal Combinations

For maximum surreal impact, combine opposing elements:
- Fire + Water: Steam puppy, mist dragon
- Earth + Air: Floating stone, wind moss
- Fire + Earth: Lava crystal, ember stone

## ASMR Considerations

Each zodiac variation should emphasize:
1. **Texture** - Close-up surface details
2. **Sound** - Material-appropriate foley
3. **Movement** - Slow, hypnotic motion
4. **Lighting** - Dramatic, mood-appropriate
