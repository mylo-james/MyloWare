# Motivational Video Style Guide

Best practices for creating motivational videos with landscape footage and quote overlays.

---

## Quote Selection Criteria

When searching for and selecting quotes online:

1. **Length**: Under 15 words - displays better on mobile screens
2. **Tone**: Positive, hopeful, empowering - avoid negative framing
3. **Universal**: Broadly relatable - avoid religious/political unless requested
4. **Timeless**: Classic wisdom resonates more than trendy phrases
5. **Match visuals**: Connect quote meaning to the landscape theme

### Quote-to-Landscape Matching

| Quote Theme | Best Landscape |
|-------------|----------------|
| Persistence, strength | Mountains, cliffs |
| Peace, calm | Ocean, lake |
| Growth, new beginnings | Forest, sunrise |
| Journey, path | Winding road, river |
| Dreams, possibilities | Sky, clouds, stars |

---

## Visual Style

### Landscape Footage
- Cinematic drone or tracking shots
- Slow motion (0.5x-0.75x speed)
- Golden hour or blue hour lighting
- No people - pure nature
- 9:16 vertical format for social

### Color Grading
- **Cinematic**: Warm highlights, cool shadows
- **Moody**: Desaturated with deep blacks
- **Vibrant**: Enhanced colors, high contrast
- **Dreamy**: Soft, lifted shadows, warm tones

### Text Overlay Style
- Large bold font (64-96px)
- White text with strong shadow
- Center or lower-third positioning
- Fade-in animation with subtle scale
- Display for 4-5 seconds

---

## Music Pairing

Match background music to the mood:

| Mood | Music Style |
|------|-------------|
| Empowering | Epic orchestral, building crescendo |
| Peaceful | Soft piano, ambient pads |
| Hopeful | Uplifting acoustic guitar |
| Dramatic | Cinematic strings, percussion |

---

## Platform Best Practices

### TikTok / Reels (9:16)
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Quote appears within first 3 seconds
- Music is essential

### YouTube Shorts
- Same as TikTok
- Can go up to 60 seconds
- Add subtle branding

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Quotes too long to read quickly
2. ❌ Text hard to read against background
3. ❌ Mismatched quote and visual mood
4. ❌ Too many quotes per video
5. ❌ Rushed pacing - let scenes breathe
